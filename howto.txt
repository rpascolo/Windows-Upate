Two options are available in order to use the Windows Update MiniTool Wrapper Script

Option one :
. copy WinUpdates.exe into the WUMT folder
. Execute WinUpdates.exe

Option two :
.copy WinUpdates.exe into a folder of your PC
. Execute WinUpdates.exe : 
	- you will be asked to browse for the WUMT folder  
	- the script copies itself into the WUMT folder
	- a shortcut is created on the desktop
	- WinUpdates.exe executes
